Who We Are

A Grassroots Movement for Harm Reduction

Naloxone Advocates Plymouth CIC is a community-led organisation committed to reducing the harms associated with substance use. We believe everyone deserves safety, dignity, and access to life‑saving information — no judgement, no barriers, just support.

How to Get Naloxone

1. Book Free Training

Attend one of our free sessions. Learn overdose recognition, naloxone use, and responder skills.

2. Get Your Kit

Receive your free naloxone kit at the end of training. Leave confident and prepared.

In an Emergency: Always call 999. Naloxone is temporary — professional medical help is essential.
